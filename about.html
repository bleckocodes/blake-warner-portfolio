About

Designer, researcher, maker

I'm a UX Designer based in the Pacific Northwest with two years of professional experience designing SaaS products and mobile interfaces — from wireframes to polished, production-ready prototypes. I graduated Summa Cum Laude with a B.A. in User Experience Design and won the UX Award for Top Student in my graduating class. Since then I've worked at Heroic Development and Supergreen, shipping real features for real users inside tight, fast-moving design cycles.

Outside of digital work, I build things. Full Mandalorian armor modeled in Blender and worn at Emerald City Comic Con. Screen-accurate blasters and props. A Rodian prosthetic that made the finals at the Boeing Museum cosplay contest. Stickers, 3D-printed gaming accessories, handmade tabletop gear. The same precision that goes into a component library goes into a painted miniature. I'm a Magic: The Gathering player, a D&D enthusiast, and a PC gamer — those communities are where my craft side lives. I'm actively looking for remote UX roles while building a creative maker business on the side.
