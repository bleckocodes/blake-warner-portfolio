About

Designer, researcher, maker

I’m a UX Designer with two years of professional experience designing SaaS products and mobile interfaces. I graduated Summa Cum Laude with a B.A. in User Experience Design from Northwest University — and I’ve spent the time since putting that education to work in real product environments.

At Heroic Development, I helped shape a board management platform from early wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes used in stakeholder demos. At Supergreen, I worked across multiple SaaS products in rapid weekly cycles, contributing to design systems, feature flows, and responsive web and mobile interfaces.

My design process is grounded in research and structured around clarity. I define the problem before I open Figma, test assumptions early, and iterate based on what real users actually do — not what I assumed they’d do. The best interfaces are the ones users never have to think about.

Outside of UX, I’m a maker. I design and 3D print custom Star Wars props and costumes, paint miniatures, play board games and Magic: The Gathering, and compete in cosplay at conventions. The same attention to detail I bring to a Mandalorian helmet, I bring to every interface I design.
